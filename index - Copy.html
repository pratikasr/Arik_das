Amazing Services

  • DCF Boxing pad work

    We believe that sparring plays a vital role in martial arts because it allows students to practice their techniques against a live moving target. Sparring is great training for stamina and resistance and the back-and-forth action mimics a more realistic life situation.

  • Boxing Gloves

    Boxers wear gloves to protect their hands against injuries. Principally, gloves aren't worn to protect the faces of the opponents, but rather to keep your own hands & knuckles safe.

  • Weight training after fighting work

    The purpose of weight training for a fighter is twofold: To improve power and explosiveness. To improve muscular endurance.
